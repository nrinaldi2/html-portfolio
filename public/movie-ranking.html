The Best Movies According to Nick

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Scarface

Incredible story about a man who pursued power so ruthlessly that it brought his ruthless demise

Goonies

Endlessly funny and engaging adventure of 4 ambitious young boys and 3 pestered teens who go on a quest to find lost treasure in their home town.

Alien

Creepy Sci-fi movie about an alien species designed to bring the end to mankind.
